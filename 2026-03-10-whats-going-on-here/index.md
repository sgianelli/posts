In my constant bid to not be dealing with social media anymore but still wanting a way to share pictures and updates with friends and family while I go about my life and have things that I think would be fun to share, I decided to hammer together a simple blog that I could just dump things to. And when I say simple, I mean simple. No user interaction or search indexing or anything like that, just simple text and images.

It's nice to have things be a little simpler every once in a while.
